Campus Life

School church

was established in 1978

The school church is a wonderful place for the students to pray. With its lovely architecture it makes a delightful place for some spiritual enlightening

School Library

was established in 1836

The school library is a charming place for students to read books. It has a high ceiling with white walls and elegant shelves. With books ranging from mystery to fantasy, adventure or even magazines and school books, the library is a place where young students can become more learned.

School Auditorium

was established in 1836

The school auditorium is a magnificient room for students to attend several events. With blue and white walls, large brown doors and a spacious stage, this hall has hosted many occasions. Most notably,Carol Evening, Independence Day and Math Mela celebrations. This theatre has a friendly atmosphere just right for students to perform in.

School Achievements

Interschool MUN
won fifth place in interschool MUN

The interschool Model United Nations was hosted by La Martiniere College Lucknow. La Martiniere for Girls won a few awards. The school hosts there own MUN once a year. The school performed well and hopes to particpate in more MUNs in the future.
